History

KillDevil, Maine's most notorious hospital for the criminally insane, was forced to close its doors nearly 60 years ago due to numerous homicides. Rumor has it that no other institution was willing to house the patients, and they remained inside the abandoned sanitarium for the rest of their lives.


In 2018, KillDevil Asylum reopened its doors and began taking new patients. There have since been a number of visitors who've gone missing, their last known whereabouts Killdevil. Could the ghosts of the those abandoned years prior be to blame?


Officials are threatening to once again shut KillDevil's doors and bar the public. Dare to visit, while you can?
