New KillDevil Corn Maze!

Legend

Legend has it that in KillDevil's early days, patients would escape the institution and hide out among the surrounding corn fields, some never to be seen again.


Since the re-opening of KillDevil, again local farmers have reported seeing patients wandering the fields at night, yet staff members say no one is missing from the asylum. We decided to clear a path to see what's going on for ourselves. Can you help us solve the mystery?
